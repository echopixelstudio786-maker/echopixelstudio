Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Echo Pixel Studio ("we", "our", or "us") develops and publishes mobile games for Android and iOS devices. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use our games, websites, and related services ("Services").

By downloading or using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Customer support requests
  • Email address (when contacting support)
  • Feedback and bug reports
  • Information submitted voluntarily

Information Collected Automatically

Our games automatically collect certain information including:

  • Device model and manufacturer
  • Operating system version
  • Language and region
  • Country and approximate location
  • IP address
  • Advertising Identifier (AAID / IDFA where applicable)
  • Crash logs and diagnostics
  • Game progress and gameplay statistics
  • Session duration
  • Purchase history
  • Ad interaction information
  • Fraud prevention signals

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us to:

  • Operate and maintain our games
  • Save game progress
  • Improve gameplay experience
  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Analyze player behavior
  • Provide customer support
  • Process in-app purchases
  • Display personalized or contextual advertisements
  • Measure advertising performance
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Advertising

Our games are offered free of charge and may display advertisements. Advertising revenue helps support ongoing game development and updates.

Advertising partners may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, approximate location, advertising identifiers, and usage information to provide relevant advertisements and measure campaign performance.

Depending on your region, advertisements may be personalized or non-personalized according to applicable privacy laws.

4. In-App Purchases

Some games offer optional in-app purchases that allow players to purchase virtual items, remove advertisements, premium content, or game currency.

All purchases are securely processed by the respective platform provider, such as Google Play or the Apple App Store. We do not receive or store your payment card information.

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging

Each third-party provider maintains its own privacy practices. We encourage users to review their respective privacy policies.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited information with trusted service providers only when necessary to:

  • Display advertisements
  • Measure ad performance
  • Provide analytics
  • Detect fraud
  • Process purchases
  • Deliver cloud services
  • Improve game performance
  • Comply with applicable laws

7. Children's Privacy

Some of our games may be designed for general audiences. Where required, we comply with Google Play Families Policy, COPPA, GDPR-K, and other applicable child privacy regulations.

When a game is directed toward children, we limit data collection, serve age-appropriate advertisements where applicable, and obtain required consent when necessary.

8. Data Security

We implement commercially reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, or disclosure.

However, no internet transmission or electronic storage method is completely secure.

9. Data Retention

Information is retained only for as long as necessary to provide our Services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed on servers located outside your country. We take appropriate safeguards to ensure data protection in accordance with applicable laws.

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:
